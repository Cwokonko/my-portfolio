Fun Facts About Me

My name is Chidera Wokonko and I'm currently an undergraduate student at Cornell University majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) with a minor in robotics. When I'm not doing problem sets or studying for an exam I'm working out, watching movies, working on my personal Internet of Things (IOT) Projects or enjoying quality time with friends. Alternatively you can find me mentoring students who are part of the CUempower organization within Diversity Programs in Engineering at Cornell University. I also run a fitness vlog on Instagram and Youtube called COFITNESS and I host weekly conversation hours at Cornell for people who want to learn the Nigerian native tongue--Igbo.
